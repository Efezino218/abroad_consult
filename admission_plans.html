Our Admission Plans

Access Plan - #25,000

Two applications maximum for any preferred course and country of your choice, plan is valid for 12 months.

This covers:

  • Consultation
  • Application until admitted

Note: Application fees may apply. In situations where a candidate's preferred school requires us to pay an application fee, we will revert to the candidates to make the payment. Application fees could vary from $50 to $150. However, we do have schools that wave these application fees.
